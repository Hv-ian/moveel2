About Moveel Lube

A California state DEF and oil delivery company built on reliability and a commitment to keeping your operations moving.

Keeping Your Operations in Motion

Moveel Lube was founded with one goal: to be the most reliable DEF and oil delivery partner in the state of California. We understand that when your equipment runs out of critical fluids, every minute of downtime costs money.

That's why we've built a statewide network of experienced drivers, regional depots, and intelligent logistics technology — so you get the right fluid, in the right quantity, at the right time.

  • Statewide delivery network
  • Experienced, certified drivers
  • ISO 22241 certified DEF products
  • 24/7 dispatch and support
